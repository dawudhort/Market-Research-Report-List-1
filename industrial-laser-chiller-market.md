<p><strong>The Industrial Laser Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-cooled Chiller</li><li>Water-cooled Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial laser chiller market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled chillers and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them more suitable for smaller applications and installations with limited water access. In contrast, water-cooled chillers rely on a continuous supply of water for cooling, offering higher efficiency and better performance in larger industrial settings. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in laser equipment, enhancing productivity and extending machinery lifespan.</p></p>
