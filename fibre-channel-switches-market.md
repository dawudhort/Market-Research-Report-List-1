<p><p>The Fibre Channel Switches market consists of two primary types: Modular Director Switches and Semi-modular Switches. Modular Director Switches, often referred to as Backbone Switches, provide high scalability and redundancy, typically used in large data centers for core connectivity. Meanwhile, Semi-modular Switches, known as Edge Switches, cater to smaller setups, connecting servers and storage devices within a network's edge. Both types are crucial for ensuring efficient data transfer, reliability, and performance in storage area networks.</p></p>
